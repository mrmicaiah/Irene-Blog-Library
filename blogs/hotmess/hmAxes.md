# THE HOT MESS — STORY GENERATION AXES

## OVERVIEW

These axes combine to generate story ideas. Select from each relevant axis, then build the premise.

**8 Axes:**
1. Story Type (slot-aware)
2. Character Focus
3. Location
4. Conflict Type
5. Emotional Tone
6. Biblical Theme
7. Time Frame
8. Season

---

## AXIS 1: STORY TYPE — SLOT-AWARE

*What shape does this story take? And which day does it run?*

### Anchor Types — Tuesday & Friday Slots

| Type | Description | Word Count |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **The Day** | Open to close. Full day with through-line. | 1,200–1,500 |
| **The Incident** | Something goes wrong. Escalation. Resolution. | 1,000–1,400 |
| **The Visitor** | Someone comes in who changes something. | 1,000–1,300 |
| **The Portrait** | Deep dive on one recurring character. | 1,200–1,500 |
| **The Milestone** | Anniversary, achievement, marking time. | 1,000–1,400 |
| **The Flashback** | Present triggers memory. Past and present braided. | 1,000–1,400 |

### Flex Types — Sunday Slots

| Type | Description | Word Count |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **The Moment** | Single scene, fully rendered. One interaction. | 800–1,000 |
| **The Letter** | Direct address to someone. | 800–1,200 |
| **The Quiet** | Nothing dramatic. Stillness. Presence. | 800–1,000 |

**6 anchor options, 3 flex options. Match the type pool to the slot.**

## HOW TO USE

### Step 1: Determine Slot
Which slot is this story for?
- **Tuesday or Friday** → choose from anchor types only
- **Sunday** → choose from flex types only

### Step 2: Run Pre-Generation Check
Review `hmStoryLog.md` and rotation rules in `hmMaster.md`.
- What characters are forbidden (last 2)?
- What types/tones are forbidden (last 2)?
- What themes are forbidden (last 3)?
- What's neglected and due?

### Step 3: Select Axes
Choose from each axis based on what's allowed and what's needed:

```
Slot: [Tuesday / Friday / Sunday]
Story Type: [Selection from correct pool]
Character Focus: [Selection]
Location: [Selection]
Conflict Type: [Selection]
Emotional Tone: [Selection]
Biblical Theme: [Selection]
Time Frame: [Selection]
Season: [Selection]
```

### Step 4: Build the Seed

```
**STORY SEED**

**Slot:** [Tuesday / Friday / Sunday]

**Axis Selections:**
- Type: [Selected]
- Character: [Selected]
- Location: [Selected]
- Conflict: [Selected]
- Tone: [Selected]
- Theme: [Selected]
- Time Frame: [Selected]
- Season: [Selected]

**Premise:** [2-3 sentences describing the story]

**Key Beats:**
1. [First major moment]
2. [Second major moment]
3. [Third major moment]
4. [Resolution/landing]

**The Lesson (Hidden):** [What Rena learns—not stated explicitly in story]

**Signature Moment:** [The Rena chaos/heart combination]

**Coffee Detail:** [Specific drink, roast, or origin to include]

**Scripture Touchpoint:** [Verse that appears or echoes—may be subtle]
```

### Step 5: Get Approval
Present the seed. Wait for user approval before drafting.

## ROTATION RULES QUICK REFERENCE

**The Core Rule — Coffee Shop First:**
At least 4 out of every 8 stories must be customer-focused or town/neighborhood-focused.

**Slot Rules:**
- Anchor types (Day, Incident, Visitor, Portrait, Milestone, Flashback) → Tue/Fri only
- Flex types (Quiet, Letter, Moment) → Sun only
- Never schedule wrong-type story in wrong slot

**Can't Repeat Within:**
- 2 stories: Character focus
- 2 stories: Story type, emotional tone
- 3 stories: Biblical theme

**Maximum Frequency:**
- Grace theme: 1 per 6 stories
- Todd Question conflict: 1 per 6 stories
- The Incident type: 2 per month (out of ~8 anchor slots)
- The Letter type: 1 per month (out of ~4 flex slots)
- The Flashback type: 1 per month
- Melancholy tone: 1 per 6 stories
- Triumphant tone: 1 per 8 stories
- Internal Only conflict: 1 per 8 stories
- Mabel focus: 1 per 6 stories
- **Family combined (David, Linda, Grandmother flashbacks): 2 per 10 stories**

**Minimum Frequency:**
- **Customers & Town: 4 out of every 8 stories**
- **New Customer: 1 per 6 stories**
- The Quiet type: rotates naturally on Sundays (~2/month)
- Family Complication: at least 1 per 10 stories
- Todd and Jennifer: 1 per 8 stories each
- David and Linda: 1 per 10 stories
- Non-shop location: at least 1 per 5 stories (max 4 shop stories in a row)
- Every theme: at least 1 per 18 stories (full rotation)
