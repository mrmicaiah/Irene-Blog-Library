# Irene Grace Daniels Blog Framework
## The Five Pillars

---

## OVERVIEW

Every post on irenegdaniels.com filters through one of five pillars. Together, they paint a complete picture of faith without filter.

**The Core Lens:** The gap between expectation and reality, covered by grace.

---

## THE FIVE PILLARS

### PILLAR 1: THE REAL
**Unfiltered life moments**

This is where you name the mess, the struggle, the "I didn't expect this" moments. The gap between expectation and reality. Not complaining - just telling the truth about what life actually looks like.

**Purpose:** To make readers feel seen. To say "me too" before you say anything else.

**Topics that fit here:**
- The hard parts of motherhood nobody warned you about
- Marriage realities vs. romantic fiction lies
- Faith struggles and doubts
- The weird limbo seasons of life
- When things don't go according to plan

**The boundary:** You share the struggle, not the spectacle. Honest, not exhibitionist.

---

### PILLAR 2: THE WORD
**Scripture that meets you where you are**

This is where you share insights from your time in the Word. Not seminary lectures - just what God showed you this week. The "water of the word" washing you clean.

**Purpose:** To ground everything in Scripture. To show how the Bible speaks to real life.

**Topics that fit here:**
- What a passage meant to you this week
- How a verse hit different in your current season
- Word studies that opened something up
- The Scripture that is anchoring you right now

**The rule:** KJV only. No exceptions. This is your brand.

---

### PILLAR 3: THE GRACE
**The covering that changes everything**

This is where you unpack how grace applies to the mess. No shame, no condemnation - just the blood that already covered it. The freedom of "I am not who I was, and I am not who I will be."

**Purpose:** To remind readers they are covered. To kill shame before it takes root.

**Topics that fit here:**
- How grace meets specific struggles
- Identity in Christ vs. identity in performance
- The difference between conviction and condemnation
- Freedom from past mistakes
- The gentle washing of the Word (not the harsh scrubbing of religion)

**The theology:** All sins committed were committed after the cross and are covered by the blood. God is gently washing you by the water of the word. No shame, just gently urging to seek His face.

---

### PILLAR 4: THE BEHIND
**Author life, behind the curtain**

This is where you share the writing process, book updates, and creative struggles. It connects readers to you as an author, not just a blogger.

**Purpose:** To build your author platform. To let readers into the journey.

**Topics that fit here:**
- Writing struggles and breakthroughs
- Book updates and behind-the-scenes
- The creative process
- What you are learning as an author
- The business side of writing (when relevant)

**The connection:** Even readers who are not writers can relate to creative struggles, discipline, and the gap between vision and reality.

---

### PILLAR 5: THE LIGHT
**Joy, humor, and celebration**

This is where you share the funny stuff, the wins, the "God showed up" moments. It keeps the blog from becoming heavy.

**Purpose:** To balance the weight. To show that faith includes joy.

**Topics that fit here:**
- Funny life moments
- Answered prayers and celebrations
- The small blessings in ordinary days
- Humor about the absurdity of life
- Living in want AND abundance without poor-mouth or pride

**The balance:** You can name hard things AND celebrate good things. Both are true.

---

## WHY THESE FIVE

These pillars prevent the blog from tilting too far in any direction:

- THE REAL without THE GRACE would be depressing
- THE GRACE without THE REAL would feel fake
- THE LIGHT keeps things from getting heavy
- THE WORD keeps it grounded in Scripture, not just opinion
- THE BEHIND builds your author platform

Together, they give you infinite content combinations while keeping the "faith without filter" promise.

---

## PILLAR COMBINATIONS

Most posts will touch multiple pillars. Common combinations:

| Primary | Secondary | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| THE REAL | THE GRACE | "Marriage is hard, but grace covers it" |
| THE WORD | THE REAL | "This verse hit me in the carpool line" |
| THE BEHIND | THE LIGHT | "I deleted 3,000 words and it was hilarious" |
| THE GRACE | THE WORD | "Here is what Scripture says about shame" |
| THE LIGHT | THE REAL | "When the funny moment revealed the hard truth" |

The fingerprint system (see igdIdeaLog.md) tracks which pillar each post primarily serves.
